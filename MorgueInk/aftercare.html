What is Tattoo/Piercing Aftercare?

Tattoo/piercing aftercare is essential to ensure that your fresh ink or new piercing heals safely and properly. Proper aftercare of both tattoos and piercings consist of regular cleaning/washing of the wound site.

Tattoo Aftercare•Do's and Don'ts

Do's Don'ts

Gently wash tattoo with unscented soap 2-3 times daily and PAT dry

Apply a tattoo-specific ointment or unscented lotion 2-3 times daily

Wear a light layer of sunscreen and protective clothing

Wash hands before touching a healing tattoo

Do not wipe/scratch a tattoo while it is healing

Do not use Aquaphor

Avoid prolonged sun exposure

Do not swim for 2 weeks after getting a tattoo

*These are general guidlines•ALWAYS ask your artist for specific aftercare instructions

Piercing Aftercare•Do's and Don'ts

Do's Don'ts

Clean piercings with a saline solution

Clean piercings 2-3 times daily

Wash hands before touching a healing piercing

Do not switch out a piercing before 2 months

Do not touch/mess with a healing piercing

Do not swim for 2 weeks after getting a tattoo

*These are general guidlines•ALWAYS ask your artist for specific aftercare instructions
